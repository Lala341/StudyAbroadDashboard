PISA assesses global educational systems, measuring performance in math, science, and reading. [source]


Definition

Percentage or number of children taking part in a reading / mathematics / science assessment passing four proficiency levels of increasing difficulty.


Educational Level

End of lower secondary


Data source: WIDE (2023)

The World Inequality Database on Education (WIDE) is a platform consolidating data from global surveys. Operating in over 170 countries, WIDE enables users to compare educational outcomes globally and within nations, considering factors like wealth, gender, ethnicity, and location that contribute to inequality.
